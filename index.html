Classic Donuts, Exceptionally Made

Since 2010 Dandy's has been bringing you the best homemade donuts the Hudson Valley has to offer. After starting as a small booth at the Kingston Farmer's Market on Saturdays we had the opportunity to open shop a year later in Kingston's Historic Stockade District. Since then we've gained a loyal following of Dandy's lovers from near and far and we were able to open a second location in New Paltz.


We believe in using quality ingredients and making our donuts fresh every morning.
